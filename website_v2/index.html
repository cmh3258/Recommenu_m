Find the closest Restaurant

Recommenu uses your current location to find the restaurant you are at, or close by. This will allow you to pull up the restaurants menu instantly or view it when you are in line or even before you get there.

Individual Item Ratings

As food lovers, we want everyone to be able to get good food every time they eat at a restaurant. With individual item ratings you can now see how each item rates according to the community around you, helping you make a more informed decision and spend your money wisely.

Social Media

In a normal restaurant experience, you either ask your friends you are with how the food is or ask the waiter what their favorite dish is. Their isn't much help on choosing something great and worth your money. Recommenu bridges this gap. Connecting with your friends and family is one of the highest priorities in today's society. Through Recommenu you can see what your friends, family, foodies and everyone else have rated. Connect through Facebook and start exploring.

Help your friends

We all love to help people. We love to help others make the most informed decisions. With Recommenu you can now help your friends and your community answer one of the most important questions, "What should I eat? What is good here?". Everyone will be able to see your ratings and if you are connected with Facebook, you will be able to see what your friends have liked.
